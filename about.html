About Me

Hi, I'm Alexis!

Hi, I’m Alexis Lewis—a digital media professional with a passion for clean design, thoughtful storytelling, and creating online experiences that feel both functional and human.

I graduated in May 2025 with a Bachelor of Applied Science in Digital Media, specializing in Web Development. During school, I completed an internship where I focused primarily on web maintenance and SEO. While the role didn’t offer many creative opportunities, it taught me how to work within real-world constraints, prioritize user needs, and maintain strong digital foundations—skills I now carry into every project.

I’m especially drawn to building websites that feel unique and original—outside the box, but still guided by industry best practices. I enjoy designing with purpose and personality, steering clear of cookie-cutter templates in favor of thoughtful, engaging digital spaces that stand out.

Outside of work, I’m a proud animal lover (especially dogs!) and bring that same loyalty, energy, and curiosity into everything I do.

Thanks for stopping by—feel free to check out my work and connect if you'd like to collaborate or just say hello!
